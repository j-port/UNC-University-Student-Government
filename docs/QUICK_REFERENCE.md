### What They Do

The four colored cards at the top (Total, Pending, In Progress, Resolved) are now **clickable filter buttons**.

### How to Use

1. **Click any card** to filter feedback by that status
2. **Active card** highlights with solid color and white text
3. **Click "Total"** to show all feedback again

### Visual Indicators

-   **Default:** Light pastel background
-   **Active:** Solid dark background, white text, bottom indicator bar
-   **Hover:** Card lifts and scales up slightly
