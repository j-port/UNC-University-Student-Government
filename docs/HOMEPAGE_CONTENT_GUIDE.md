## ğŸ¨ Content Writing Tips

### For Statistics:

-   Use "+" for ranges (e.g., "10,000+")
-   Add currency symbols (â‚±, $)
-   Keep it short (max 10 characters)

### For Core Values:

-   One or two words max
-   Action-oriented (e.g., "Service", not "We serve")
-   Keep it professional and inspiring

### For Descriptions:

-   Write in present tense
-   Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences)
-   Be clear and concise
-   Proofread before saving!

### For Titles:

-   Use Title Case (Capitalize Each Word)
-   Be descriptive but brief
-   Avoid abbreviations unless widely known
